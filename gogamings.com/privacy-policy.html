Guide to Responsible Play

Sometimes, players may find it hard to recognize that their online play is getting out of control. A common reaction is to minimize, hide or deny such problems and the harm it could be causing. Some players lie to themselves about how much money or time is being spent on online playing.

Following are some of the best practices to help you play responsibly:
  • • Play online games in moderation and only for entertainment.
  • • Do not play online games to make money or escape problems.
  • • Never chase your losses while playing such games.
  • • Set aside an entertainment budget for playing online games.
  • • Balance the time you spend on playing online games with other leisure activities.
  • • Recognize whether you are not playing responsibly.
